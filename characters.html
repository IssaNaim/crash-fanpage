The Game Characters

Crash

Crash Bandicoot is the title character and main protagonist of the Crash Bandicoot series. Introduced in the 1996 video game Crash Bandicoot, Crash is a mutant eastern barred bandicoot who was genetically engineered through the use of Cortex and Doctor Nitrus Brio's Evolvo-Ray.

Doctor Neo Cortex

Doctor Neo Cortex is a fictional character and the main antagonist of the Crash Bandicoot video game series. He has appeared in every mainline game in the series as Crash Bandicoot's archenemy, as well as a playable character in several spin-off titles.

Uka Uka

Uka Uka is a voodoo mask spirit, Aku Aku's twin brother, and the overarching antagonist of the Crash Bandicoot series. He is ultimately the driving force behind Neo Cortex's evil plans, though he is short-tempered and often blames him for their failures.

Tawna Bandicoot

Tawna Bandicoot is a character that appeared in the first Crash Bandicoot game and later became a recurring character in the series following the release of Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy. She is a feminine anthropomorphic bandicoot that appears in the first Crash Bandicoot game as a creation of Dr. Neo Cortex and the girlfriend of Crash Bandicoot, who rescues her from the former in the game.

Aku Aku

Aku Aku is a witch doctor spirit living inside a wooden mask who guides Crash, his sister Coco and their friends as they defeat the forces of evil. In most platforming Crash games, if 3 masks are found, Crash becomes invincible for a limited amount of time, with Aku Aku being placed on Crash's face during this period. He is also a father figure to Crash, Coco, and their friends.
